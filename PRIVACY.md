# Privacy Policy — Claude Tracker

**Last updated: July 2026**

Claude Tracker does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any user data.

## What the extension does

- Reads Claude usage information (session/weekly limits, token estimates)
  directly from claude.ai pages you visit, entirely on your device.
- Renders that information as an inline panel on the page.
- Stores your preferences (language, bar color, layout) in your browser's
  local storage, on your device only.

## What the extension does NOT do

- No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry.
- No user data ever leaves your device.
- No accounts, no sign-in.
- No cookies set by the extension.

## Network requests

The extension makes exactly one type of outbound request: it fetches a static
`meta.json` file from this project's GitHub Pages site
(`markussela.github.io`) to display up-to-date author information and the
project funding goal inside the settings panel. This request contains no user
data, no identifiers, and nothing about your Claude usage.
